Welcome to Mozilla U.P Community

The Mozilla Uttar Pradesh community is part of the global Mozilla community, primarily aimed at spreading the mission of Mozilla in Uttar Pradesh. This website is for all the contributors across the state and will also help us in collaborating on projects and spread awareness about Mozilla Initiatives.
